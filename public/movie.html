Best Movies According to Chandramouli

Top 3 Movies of all time

Aashiqui 2

A successful singer falls in love with a bar singer, and hopes to make her a star. Can he do that?

Chichhore

A boy attempts to commit suicide due to not qualifying for the JEE. His father and the father's friends tell their tale of success and failure in college to motivate him.

The Kashmir Files

A true tale of how Kashmiri Hindus were given three options: leave, convert, or die.
